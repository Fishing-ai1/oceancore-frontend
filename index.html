Beta Launch Checklist

Use this before sending the app to more testers.

Manual phone test

1. Login

Sign out, sign back in, close browser, reopen, confirm it stays signed in.

2. Catch Log

Save a catch with a photo, refresh, then test Share Card and Delete catch.

3. Map & Saved Areas

Use GPS, load conditions, save an area, reload that area, then ask AI about it.

Launch safety

Spot Safe

Catch cards and community features must never show exact GPS publicly.

Legal & Safety

Keep marine disclaimer visible. AI advice is support only, not official forecast/navigation.
